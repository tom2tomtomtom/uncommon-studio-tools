# /crisis-response

Create an immediate crisis response plan.

## What this does
Generates a complete crisis response plan with holding statements, stakeholder priorities, and an action timeline for the first 48 hours.

## When to use
- Breaking news or emerging issue
- Client facing sudden backlash
- Need to act fast but smart

## How to use
Just tell me about the crisis situation. Include:
- What happened
- Who's affected
- What you know so far

## Example
```
/crisis-response

A product recall was announced this morning. Customer complaints are flooding social media. The CEO wants to know our response plan within 2 hours.
```

## What you'll get
1. **Holding statement** - Ready to post/share immediately
2. **First 4 hours** - Critical actions and who does what
3. **Stakeholder sequence** - Who to tell, in what order
4. **Message framework** - Core narrative and key talking points
5. **Next 48 hours** - Action timeline with milestones
