# /social-posts

Create social media posts for multiple platforms.

## What this does
Generates platform-optimized posts for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook - each adapted for that platform's audience and format.

## When to use
- Need to repurpose content across platforms
- Launching something and need social support
- Weekly content creation
- Promoting a piece of content

## How to use
Share:
- What you're promoting/talking about
- Which platforms you need
- Tone/voice (if specific)
- Any hashtags or handles to include

## Example
```
/social-posts

Promoting our new whitepaper: "The Future of Remote Work"
Key stats: 73% of workers prefer hybrid, productivity up 15%
Platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram
Tone: Thought leadership but not boring
Include link to download
```

## What you'll get
For each platform:
- **Primary post** - Optimized for that platform's format
- **Alternate version** - Different hook or angle
- **Best time to post** - Platform-specific recommendations
- **Hashtag suggestions** - Relevant, not spammy
- **Engagement prompt** - How to spark conversation
