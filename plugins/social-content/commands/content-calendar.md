# /content-calendar

Plan a content calendar for any time period.

## What this does
Creates a strategic content calendar with themes, topics, and posting schedule - not just random ideas, but a coherent plan.

## When to use
- Monthly or quarterly content planning
- New client onboarding
- Need to get organized
- Presenting content strategy to stakeholders

## How to use
Share:
- Time period (week, month, quarter)
- Platforms/channels
- Content pillars or themes
- Any key dates or events
- Posting frequency goals

## Example
```
/content-calendar

Month: April
Brand: B2B SaaS for HR teams
Platforms: LinkedIn (3x/week), Blog (2x/month), Newsletter (weekly)
Themes: Employee experience, HR tech, Company culture
Key dates: HR Tech Conference April 15-17
Goal: Drive signups for our conference booth
```

## What you'll get
- **Monthly overview** - Themes and focus for the period
- **Weekly breakdown** - Specific topics and formats
- **Content mix** - Balance of educational, promotional, engaging
- **Key dates highlighted** - What to post when
- **Content briefs** - Quick descriptions for each piece
