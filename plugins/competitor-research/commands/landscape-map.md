# /landscape-map

Map the competitive landscape and see where you stand. Takes ~5 minutes.

## What this does
Creates a structured side-by-side comparison of your product against competitors — covering positioning, strengths, weaknesses, pricing, and target users.

## When to use
- Starting competitive research from scratch
- Preparing a pitch or strategy presentation
- Need a clear picture of where you fit in the market
- Onboarding a new team member on the competitive landscape

## How to use
Share:
- Your product/app (name, URL, or description)
- 2-5 competitors to map (names or URLs)
- What market or category you're in
- Any specific dimensions you care about (pricing, features, UX, messaging)

## Example
```
/landscape-map

Our app: TaskFlow (project management for creative teams) — taskflow.io
Competitors:
- Asana (asana.com)
- Monday.com (monday.com)
- Basecamp (basecamp.com)
- ClickUp (clickup.com)

We focus on creative workflows specifically. Help me see where we stand.
```

## What you'll get
- **Landscape comparison table** — Side-by-side on key dimensions
- **Positioning map** — Where each player sits on the axes that matter
- **Your advantages** — Where you're genuinely ahead
- **Your vulnerabilities** — Where competitors are stronger
- **White space** — Positions no one owns that you could claim
- **Key takeaway** — The one insight that matters most
