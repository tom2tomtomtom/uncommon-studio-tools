# /client-health

Assess client relationship health and get recommendations.

## What this does
Analyzes signals about your client relationship and flags risks early, with specific recommendations to strengthen the partnership.

## When to use
- Quarterly account reviews
- Something feels "off" with a client
- Preparing for renewal conversation
- New team member taking over account

## How to use
Share what you know:
- Recent interactions (meetings, emails)
- Project status and feedback
- Any changes (new contacts, budget shifts)
- Your gut feeling

## Example
```
/client-health

Client: TechCorp (18 months, $400k/year)
Recent signals:
- Meeting attendance dropped (used to be CEO + CMO, now just CMO)
- Last project feedback was "fine" (used to be enthusiastic)
- They asked about pricing for next year (never asked before)
- Heard they're hiring an in-house creative director
- Our main champion Sarah seems stressed lately
- They pushed back phase 2 start by 6 weeks
```

## What you'll get
1. **Health score** - Red/yellow/green with rationale
2. **Risk signals** - What's concerning and why
3. **Root cause hypotheses** - What might be driving this
4. **Recommended actions** - Specific steps, prioritized
5. **Conversation starters** - How to address concerns directly
6. **Upsell opportunities** - If relationship is strong
