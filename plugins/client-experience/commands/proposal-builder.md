# /proposal-builder

Create or improve client proposals.

## What this does
Helps you structure proposals, write compelling sections, and find relevant case studies or proof points from past work.

## When to use
- Starting a new proposal from scratch
- Need to improve weak sections
- Want to find relevant past work
- Stuck on how to position your approach

## How to use
Share:
- Client brief or RFP
- What you're proposing
- Your approach/methodology
- Budget range (if known)
- Past similar work (if relevant)

## Example
```
/proposal-builder

Client: Regional healthcare system
Need: Complete rebrand including visual identity, messaging, and brand guidelines
Budget: $200-250k
Timeline: 6 months
Competition: They're also talking to 2 other agencies
Our edge: We've done 5+ healthcare rebrands including [past client]
Sections I need help with: Approach, timeline, why us
```

## What you'll get
- **Executive summary** - Hook them in the first page
- **Approach section** - Methodology that feels tailored
- **Timeline** - Realistic phases and milestones
- **Team/capabilities** - Why you're the right choice
- **Investment section** - How to present pricing
- **Relevant proof** - Past work to reference
