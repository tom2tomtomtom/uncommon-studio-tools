# /meeting-notes

Transform meeting notes into organized summaries with action items.

## What this does
Takes your raw meeting notes, transcript, or even a photo of handwritten notes and turns them into a clean summary with decisions, action items, and follow-up email draft.

## When to use
- After any client meeting
- Processing workshop notes
- Need to share decisions with stakeholders
- Want to never lose an action item again

## How to use
Paste or share:
- Meeting notes (messy is fine)
- Transcript from Zoom/Teams
- Voice memo transcript
- Photo of whiteboard or handwritten notes

## Example
```
/meeting-notes

Meeting with Acme Corp - brand refresh kickoff
- CEO wants bold, not conservative
- Concerned about timeline (Q3 launch)
- Loves competitor X's approach but different
- Budget ~$150k for phase 1
- Need to see 3 concepts by end of month
- Sarah is main POC, but CEO wants to approve
- They're presenting to board in June
- Mentioned potential Phase 2 for website
```

## What you'll get
1. **Meeting summary** - Key discussion points, organized
2. **Decisions made** - What was agreed
3. **Action items** - Who, what, when (clearly assigned)
4. **Open questions** - What still needs answering
5. **Follow-up email draft** - Ready to send to attendees
