# /production-brief

Create a comprehensive production brief for video, photo, or print projects.

## What this does
Generates a detailed production brief that aligns creative, client, and production teams on scope, budget, timeline, and deliverables.

## When to use
- Kicking off a new production project
- Getting alignment before bidding vendors
- Documenting scope to prevent creep
- Creating RFP/RFQ documents

## How to use
Share:
- What type of production (video, photo, print)
- What the creative concept is
- Budget range (if known)
- Timeline/deadline
- Deliverables needed
- Any special requirements

## Example
```
/production-brief

Video production for new product launch. We need a :30 TV spot and cutdowns for social. Concept is approved - product hero shots with lifestyle moments. Budget around $300K. Need to launch in 8 weeks. Will air nationally plus digital.
```

## What you'll get
- **Project Overview** - Objectives and creative summary
- **Deliverables Matrix** - All assets with specs
- **Budget Framework** - Category breakdown
- **Timeline** - Key milestones and dates
- **Technical Requirements** - Specs, usage, platforms
- **Team & Approvals** - Who's involved at each stage
- **Risks & Considerations** - What to watch for
