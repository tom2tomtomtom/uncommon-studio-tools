# /media-pitch

Craft a targeted journalist pitch email.

## What this does
Creates a personalized, compelling pitch email designed for a specific journalist or media outlet, with a clear news angle and call to action.

## When to use
- Pitching a story to specific journalists
- Offering exclusive access or data
- Following up on a press release with targeted outreach
- Securing feature or profile coverage

## How to use
Share:
- The story angle
- Target journalist/outlet/beat
- Why it matters to their audience
- What you're offering (interview, data, exclusive)
- Timeline and any news hooks

## Example
```
/media-pitch

Story: CEO of our tech client has a unique perspective on AI regulation
(former regulator turned startup founder)
Target: Tech policy reporters at The Verge, Wired, Ars Technica
Why now: EU AI Act implementation beginning next quarter
Offering: Exclusive interview with CEO, plus proprietary survey data
on developer sentiment about AI regulation
```

## What you'll get
1. **Subject line options** - 3 variations optimized for open rates
2. **Full pitch email** - Hook, context, proof, ask
3. **Personalization notes** - How to tailor for specific journalists
4. **Follow-up strategy** - Timing and approach for follow-ups
5. **Supporting materials list** - What to have ready if they respond
