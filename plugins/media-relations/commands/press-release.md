# /press-release

Write a professional press release in AP style.

## What this does
Drafts a complete press release following AP style guidelines and journalism conventions, with proper structure, quotes, and boilerplate.

## When to use
- Announcing company news, product launches, or partnerships
- Sharing research findings or survey results
- Executive appointments or organizational changes
- Event announcements or milestone celebrations

## How to use
Share:
- The news (what's being announced)
- Key details (who, when, where, why)
- Spokesperson name and title
- Target media (trade, national, local)
- Any supporting data or quotes

## Example
```
/press-release

News: Our client (sustainable packaging startup) just raised $25M Series B
Key details: Led by Sequoia, brings total funding to $40M, will expand to Europe
Spokesperson: CEO Jane Smith
Target: Business press + sustainability trades
Data: Their packaging has diverted 50M pounds of plastic from landfills
```

## What you'll get
1. **Headline and subheadline** - Clear, newsworthy, AP style
2. **Full press release** - Proper structure with inverted pyramid
3. **Spokesperson quote** - Natural, quotable, on-message
4. **Boilerplate** - Company description paragraph
5. **Distribution notes** - Timing and targeting recommendations
