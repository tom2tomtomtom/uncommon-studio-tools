# /coverage-analysis

Analyze media coverage and extract insights.

## What this does
Evaluates media coverage for patterns, sentiment, message pull-through, and strategic opportunities, providing actionable recommendations.

## When to use
- Monthly or quarterly coverage reporting
- Post-campaign media analysis
- Competitive share of voice assessment
- Crisis coverage evaluation

## How to use
Share:
- Coverage to analyze (list of articles, clips, or descriptions)
- Time period
- Key messages you were trying to land
- Competitors to compare against
- Reporting audience (client, exec, team)

## Example
```
/coverage-analysis

Period: Q4 2024
Client: Enterprise SaaS company
Coverage: 45 mentions total - 12 in tech trade (TechCrunch, VentureBeat),
8 in business press (Forbes, Inc), 25 in industry blogs
Key messages: 1) AI-powered automation 2) 40% cost reduction 3) Enterprise-grade security
Competitors: Salesforce, HubSpot, Monday.com
Audience: Client VP of Communications
```

## What you'll get
1. **Coverage summary** - Volume, tier breakdown, trends
2. **Sentiment analysis** - Positive/neutral/negative with examples
3. **Message pull-through** - Which messages landed and where
4. **Competitive comparison** - Share of voice and positioning
5. **Strategic recommendations** - What to do differently next quarter
