# /coverage-analysis

Analyze media coverage and extract insights.

## What this does
Reviews media coverage and gives you a clear summary: sentiment, key messages that landed, what's missing, and strategic recommendations.

## When to use
- After a launch or announcement
- Monthly/quarterly media reviews
- Competitive coverage analysis
- Proving PR value to stakeholders

## How to use
Share or paste:
- Article links, headlines, or full text
- What you were trying to achieve
- Timeframe being analyzed

## Example
```
/coverage-analysis

Here are 12 articles from our product launch last week:
[paste headlines/summaries or links]

Our key messages were:
1. First AI-powered solution in our industry
2. 50% faster than competitors
3. Already used by Fortune 500 companies

Goal: Drive awareness and position us as the innovation leader.
```

## What you'll get
- **Sentiment breakdown** - Positive/neutral/negative split
- **Message pickup** - Which key messages got coverage
- **Top-performing angles** - What resonated most
- **Coverage gaps** - What was missed or misunderstood
- **Share of voice** - vs. competitors if applicable
- **Recommendations** - What to do next
