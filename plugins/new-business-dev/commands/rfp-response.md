# /rfp-response

Craft a strategic RFP response.

## What this does
Develops a structured RFP response with executive summary, approach, team, case studies, and commercial framework.

## When to use
- Received a formal RFP to respond to
- Need to structure a written proposal
- Want to differentiate in a competitive process
- Deadline approaching and need to move fast

## How to use
Share:
- The RFP requirements (or summarize key sections)
- Your agency's relevant capabilities
- Relevant case studies or past work
- Team members you'd propose
- Budget range or commercial parameters

## Example
```
/rfp-response

RFP: State tourism board seeking agency for 2-year destination marketing campaign
Requirements: Brand refresh, digital campaign, social media management, influencer program
Budget: $3M over 2 years
Our strengths: Tourism experience (3 CVBs), strong digital/social team
Team: We'd put our top CD and a dedicated strategist on it
Key differentiator: Proprietary social listening tool for tourism sentiment
```

## What you'll get
1. **Executive summary** - Compelling overview that wins the skim
2. **Approach section** - Strategic and creative methodology
3. **Team structure** - Roles, bios, and relevant experience
4. **Case study framework** - How to present relevant work
5. **Differentiation strategy** - What to emphasize and why
