# /prospect-brief

Research and brief on a prospect before a meeting.

## What this does
Creates a comprehensive prospect brief covering company background, marketing assessment, opportunities, and conversation starters for your meeting.

## When to use
- Preparing for a credentials meeting
- Researching a potential lead
- Briefing the team before a pitch
- Evaluating whether to pursue an opportunity

## How to use
Share:
- Company/brand name
- What you know about the opportunity
- Any contacts or decision-makers
- Your agency's relevant capabilities
- Type of meeting (credentials, chemistry, pitch)

## Example
```
/prospect-brief

Company: Allbirds
Context: Their CMO posted on LinkedIn about reviewing agency partners
Meeting type: Initial credentials/chemistry call
Our angle: We have DTC experience and sustainability brand expertise
Contact: Marketing Director (connected via industry event)
```

## What you'll get
1. **Company overview** - Business model, size, recent developments
2. **Marketing assessment** - Current brand, campaigns, digital presence
3. **Opportunity analysis** - Why they might be looking and what they need
4. **Competitive landscape** - Who else might be pursuing them
5. **Conversation starters** - Specific observations and smart questions to ask
