# /pitch-deck

Structure a compelling pitch deck for new business.

## What this does
Creates a strategic pitch deck outline with narrative arc, key messages per slide, and supporting content recommendations.

## When to use
- Preparing for a competitive pitch
- Responding to a brief with a presentation
- Building credentials for a prospect meeting
- Restructuring an existing deck that isn't landing

## How to use
Share:
- Prospect name and industry
- What they're looking for
- Your agency's key differentiators
- Any brief or RFP details
- Who will be in the room

## Example
```
/pitch-deck

Prospect: Regional health system launching direct-to-consumer telehealth
Looking for: Brand and digital agency for launch campaign
Our strengths: Healthcare experience, integrated creative+digital
Brief: $1.5M campaign, launch in Q3, target adults 30-55
Audience: CMO, VP Digital, Head of Telehealth
```

## What you'll get
1. **Narrative arc** - Story structure from opening to close
2. **Slide-by-slide outline** - Key message and content per slide
3. **Insight/provocation** - The hook that makes your pitch memorable
4. **Case study recommendations** - Which work to show and why
5. **Q&A prep** - Likely questions and suggested responses
