# /email-campaign

Design a strategic email campaign or sequence.

## What this does
Creates a complete email campaign plan with audience segmentation, email sequence, subject lines, content outlines, and success metrics.

## When to use
- Launching a new product or service
- Setting up automated email sequences
- Re-engaging lapsed subscribers
- Planning seasonal or promotional campaigns

## How to use
Share:
- Campaign goal (awareness, conversion, retention)
- Target audience and any segmentation
- Product/service being promoted
- Timeline and number of emails
- Any brand voice guidelines

## Example
```
/email-campaign

Goal: Welcome sequence for new free trial signups
Product: Project management SaaS for creative agencies
Audience: Agency owners who just signed up for 14-day trial
Emails: 5-email sequence over 14 days
Voice: Friendly, practical, not salesy
Goal: Convert free trial to paid plan
```

## What you'll get
1. **Campaign strategy** - Overall approach and goals
2. **Email sequence** - Timing, subject lines, and purpose of each email
3. **Content outlines** - Key messages and CTAs per email
4. **Segmentation plan** - How to personalize based on behavior
5. **Success metrics** - KPIs and benchmarks to measure
