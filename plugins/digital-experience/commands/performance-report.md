# /performance-report

Create a digital performance report.

## What this does
Builds a structured performance report that turns metrics into insights and recommendations, suitable for clients or internal stakeholders.

## When to use
- Monthly or quarterly reporting
- Campaign post-mortem analysis
- Client performance reviews
- Executive digital performance summaries

## How to use
Share:
- Reporting period
- Channels to cover (SEO, email, social, paid)
- Key metrics and data points
- Goals or benchmarks to compare against
- Audience for the report (client, exec, team)

## Example
```
/performance-report

Period: January 2025
Channels: Organic search, email marketing, paid social
Data: 45K organic sessions (+12% MoM), email open rate 28%,
Facebook ROAS 3.2x, 150 new leads
Goals: 50K organic sessions, 30% open rate, 4x ROAS
Audience: Client marketing director
```

## What you'll get
1. **Executive summary** - Key wins, challenges, and recommendations
2. **Channel breakdown** - Performance vs. goals with trend analysis
3. **Key insights** - What the data means, not just what it shows
4. **Optimization recommendations** - Prioritized next actions
5. **Forward look** - What to expect and plan for next period
