# /positioning

Develop brand positioning and differentiation strategy.

## What this does
Creates a strategic positioning framework that defines where your brand sits in the market and why customers should choose you over competitors.

## When to use
- Starting a new brand or rebrand
- Feeling lost in a crowded market
- Competitors eating your lunch
- Need to articulate what makes you different

## How to use
Share:
- What do you do? (product/service)
- Who's your target audience?
- Who are your main competitors?
- What do you think makes you different?

## Example
```
/positioning

We're a project management app for creative agencies.
Competitors: Monday, Asana, Basecamp
Target: Small creative agencies (5-30 people)
We think we're different because: Built by agency people, creative workflow specific
```

## What you'll get
1. **Positioning statement** - The classic "For [target], [brand] is the [category] that [benefit] because [reason to believe]"
2. **Competitive positioning map** - Where you sit vs. competitors
3. **Key differentiators** - What actually sets you apart
4. **Supporting proof points** - Evidence that backs up your claims
5. **Positioning don'ts** - Claims to avoid
