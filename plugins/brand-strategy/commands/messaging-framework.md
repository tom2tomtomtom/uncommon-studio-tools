# /messaging-framework

Create a complete messaging hierarchy and framework.

## What this does
Develops a structured messaging system from brand essence down to proof points - everything your team needs to communicate consistently.

## When to use
- After defining positioning
- Team using inconsistent messaging
- Onboarding new team members or agencies
- Annual brand refresh

## How to use
Share:
- Your positioning or brand strategy
- Target audience(s)
- Key product/service benefits
- Any existing messaging to build from

## Example
```
/messaging-framework

Brand: Sustainable fashion brand for professional women
Positioning: Style that doesn't cost the earth
Audiences: 
1. Career women 30-45 who care about sustainability
2. Fashion-forward professionals who want quality
Key benefits: Timeless designs, ethical production, quality materials
```

## What you'll get
1. **Brand essence** - The core idea in 3-5 words
2. **Brand promise** - What you commit to customers
3. **Value propositions** - By audience segment
4. **Message pillars** - 3-4 key themes to always hit
5. **Proof points** - Evidence supporting each pillar
6. **Tone guidelines** - How to say it, not just what
