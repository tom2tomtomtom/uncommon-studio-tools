# /competitor-analysis

Analyze competitors and find strategic opportunities.

## What this does
Breaks down competitor positioning, messaging, and strategy to find gaps and opportunities for your brand.

## When to use
- Preparing for a pitch
- Client asks "how are we different?"
- Need competitive intelligence fast
- Strategic planning sessions

## How to use
Share:
- Your brand/client info
- 2-5 competitors to analyze
- What you know about them (websites, taglines, recent campaigns)
- What you're trying to figure out

## Example
```
/competitor-analysis

Our client: Regional bank trying to attract millennials
Competitors to analyze:
- Chime (digital-first, no fees)
- Local Credit Union (community focused)
- Big National Bank (resources, brand recognition)

What we need: Where's the white space for our client?
```

## What you'll get
- **Competitor profiles** - Positioning, strengths, weaknesses
- **Messaging analysis** - What they're saying, what's landing
- **Positioning map** - Visual of competitive landscape
- **White space opportunities** - Gaps no one owns
- **Strategic recommendations** - How to differentiate
