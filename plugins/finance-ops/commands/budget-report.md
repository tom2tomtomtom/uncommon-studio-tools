# /budget-report

Generate budget vs actual reports with variance analysis and forecasting.

## What this does
Compares actual spending against budget, identifies significant variances, spots trends, and updates forecasts. Delivers board-ready financial summaries.

## When to use
- Monthly or quarterly financial reporting
- Board meeting preparation
- Department budget reviews
- Forecast updates and reforecasting

## How to use
Share:
- Actual revenue and expense data
- Budget figures for the same period
- Prior period or prior year comparisons (if available)
- Any known one-time items or adjustments

## Example
```
/budget-report

Here's our Q4 data:

Actuals:
- Revenue: $2.4M (Budget: $2.6M)
- COGS: $960K (Budget: $1.04M)
- Marketing: $320K (Budget: $280K)
- Engineering: $580K (Budget: $550K)
- G&A: $190K (Budget: $200K)

Prior year Q4 revenue was $2.1M.
We had a one-time $40K conference sponsorship in Marketing.

Need this ready for the board meeting.
```

## What you'll get
- **Executive summary** - Key takeaways in 3-5 bullet points
- **Variance analysis** - Dollar and percentage variances with explanations
- **Trend identification** - Quarter-over-quarter and year-over-year patterns
- **Forecast updates** - Revised projections based on current run rates
